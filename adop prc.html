Our Adoption Process

Step 1: Find Your Cat

Browse through our gallery of cats and find the one that you feel would be a perfect addition to your family.

Step 2: Submit an Application

Fill out our adoption application form. We require some basic information about you, your living situation, and your experience with pets.

Step 3: Interview

Once your application is reviewed, our adoption coordinator will contact you for a brief interview. This helps us ensure that you and the cat are a good match.

Step 4: Meet and Greet

We will schedule a time for you to meet the cat. This can be done at our adoption center or, in some cases, a home visit may be arranged.

Step 5: Home Visit

For certain adoptions, we may conduct a home visit to ensure that your home is safe and suitable for the cat.

Step 6: Finalize Adoption

If everything goes well, you will sign the adoption agreement, pay the adoption fee, and take your new feline friend home.
